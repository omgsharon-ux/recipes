Notes

For a double crust pie: do not crimp edges until after topping with second crust. Trim both crusts to 3/4-inch, fold top under bottom, then crimp. Brush with egg wash and sprinkle with 1 Tbsp sugar. Cut 3–4 slits in top. Source: inspiredtaste.net
