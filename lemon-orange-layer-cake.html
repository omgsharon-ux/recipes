Notes

No cake flour? For each cup of all-purpose flour, remove 2 Tbsp and replace with 2 Tbsp cornstarch. For this recipe: measure 3 cups AP flour, remove 6 Tbsp, replace with 6 Tbsp cornstarch. Whisk to blend.

If orange cream cheese frosting is too soft, add more powdered sugar or chill as needed. This frosting pipes best when still chilled — make in advance, refrigerate, soften slightly and remix (do not microwave). Source: mycakeschool.com
