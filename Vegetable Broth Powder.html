Notes

Reconstitute with water to make a quick vegetarian stock — add 1 tablespoon of vegetable powder for every cup of boiling water. Add directly to cooking water for grains like rice, quinoa, or polenta for easy flavor. Use as a seasoning sprinkle (great on popcorn!). Blend into cream cheese for a vegetable spread. To make vegetarian gravy: add 2 tablespoons to 1 cup of water, simmer to reduce, then thicken with 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons cold water.
