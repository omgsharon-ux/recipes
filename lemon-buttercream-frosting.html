Notes

Yield: 6 cups — enough to fill and frost a 3-layer 8-inch cake. Can be frozen in an airtight container for at least 3 months; thaw on countertop and remix. If buttercream is too thin, add more powdered sugar. If too thick, add more milk 1 teaspoon at a time. Source: mycakeschool.com
