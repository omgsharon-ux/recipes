Notes

Patty Davidson's recipe. Make-Ahead / Par-Bake Option: After the Day 1 rise, shape rolls and place in the 9x13 dish without baking. Refrigerate overnight to rise. Bake at 200° for 1–1¼ hours until cooked through but not browned (par-baked). Cool on a wire rack. Freeze individually or refrigerate 1–3 days. To brown and serve: preheat oven to 350° and bake 15 minutes until golden brown. For best results, let frozen rolls thaw before baking.
