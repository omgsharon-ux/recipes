Notes

The recipe makes a little over a quart, or roughly enough to equal about 4 cans. Use any place where you would use Campbell's Condensed Cream of *something* Soup. Can be frozen.

Variations

Cream of Chicken: Add ½ cup of finely minced, cooked, dark meat chicken.

Cream of Celery: Increase celery to ½ cup of finely minced celery.

Cream of Mushroom: 1 (6.5 oz) can of sliced mushrooms, drained and finely minced.

Cream of Shrimp: Prepare base recipe and add ½ cup of cooked salad shrimp, chopped.

Single Can Roux Substitute: Prepare a light roux using 2 tablespoons butter and 3 tablespoons flour. Slowly stir in up to 1 cup of liquid (broth, water, milk) to desired consistency.
