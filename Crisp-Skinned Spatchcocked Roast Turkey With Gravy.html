Notes

Dry brine (recommended): Combine 1/2 cup Diamond Crystal kosher salt (or 6 tbsp Morton's kosher salt) with 2 tbsp baking powder. Pat turkey dry. Generously sprinkle mixture on all surfaces. Transfer to a rack on a rimmed baking sheet and refrigerate uncovered for 12–24 hours. (Up to 3 days is even better — cover loosely after 24 hours to prevent excess moisture loss.) Roast without rinsing; omit additional salt in the recipe.
